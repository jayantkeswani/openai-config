    Click, chill, delivered

    Shop your favourite brands like Nestle and Coca-Cola and schedule a delivery for a time and day that suits you or shop in a hurry with our easy Pick n Pay asap app and get same-day delivery with just a few taps on the app and get all your favourite refreshments from Ceres, Liqui-Fruit and PnP delivered directly to your door.

    Easy chilled delivery, nationwide.

    With our unbreakable cold-chain, all fresh products are packed into cold storage containers, giving you added peace of mind that your fresh food stays fresher for longer. From first click to final drop-off, you can enjoy an easy online shopping experience and get your shopping done and delivered without having to stop at the shops.

    Book a slot or choose Click n Collect for online shopping without the hassle. Browse our digital aisles and enjoy safe and secure checkout with super-smart savings and discounts when you link your Smart Shopper card to your online account. From next-day to same-day, shop Pick n Pay your way.
